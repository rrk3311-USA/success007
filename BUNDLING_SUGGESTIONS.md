## üí∞ **Pricing Strategy**

### **Bundle Discounts:**
- **2-Product Bundles:** 10-15% off
- **3-Product Bundles:** 15-20% off
- **4+ Product Bundles:** 20-25% off

### **Price Tiers:**
- **Entry Level:** $40-60 (2 products)
- **Mid Range:** $70-100 (3 products)
- **Premium:** $120+ (4+ products)

### **Psychological Pricing:**
- End prices in 7s or 9s ($47, $49, $97, $99)
- Round to nearest dollar for simplicity
- Show "Save $X" prominently
