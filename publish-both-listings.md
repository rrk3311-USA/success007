## Quick Publish Steps

### Via eBay Seller Hub (Recommended)

1. **Go to Seller Hub:**
   - Visit: https://www.ebay.com/sh/landing
   - Click **"Drafts"** or **"Unsold"** tab

2. **Find Your Listings:**
   - Search for SKU: `10777-810` (single pack)
   - Search for SKU: `10777-810-2` (2-pack)
   - Or look for "Liver Cleanse" in the listing titles

3. **For Each Listing:**
   - Click **"Edit"** or **"Revise"**
   - Scroll to **"Category"** section
   - Click **"Change category"**
   - Navigate: **Health & Beauty** → **Vitamins & Dietary Supplements**
   - Select the most specific subcategory (must be a leaf category - no subcategories)
   - Click **"Save"**
   - Click **"Publish"** or **"List item"**

4. **Verify:**
   - Check **"Active Listings"** tab
   - Both should appear as live listings
   - You'll get Listing IDs (different from Offer IDs)

## Alternative: Bulk Edit

If you see both listings:
1. Select both checkboxes
2. Click **"Bulk Edit"**
3. Change category for both at once
4. Publish both
