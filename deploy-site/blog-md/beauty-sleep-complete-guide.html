Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will I feel groggy in the morning?

A: Most users wake feeling refreshed, not groggy. Morning grogginess can occur if you don't allow adequate sleep time (need 7-8 hour window) or if you're particularly sensitive. If this occurs, try taking earlier or reduce dose slightly.

Q: Can I take this every night?

A: Yes, Beauty Sleep Pill is formulated for regular use. The ingredients support your body's natural processes rather than forcing sedation. However, periodically assess if you still need it or if improved sleep hygiene allows less frequent use.

Q: Is this habit-forming?

A: No, these ingredients are not habit-forming like prescription sleep medications. You won't experience withdrawal or rebound insomnia when you stop.

Q: Can I take this with my antidepressant?

A: Consult your psychiatrist before combining 5-HTP with antidepressants, especially SSRIs. There's theoretical risk of serotonin syndrome, though rare. Your doctor can advise on safety.

Q: Will this really improve my skin?

A: Quality sleep is when your body repairs and renews skin. While this isn't a topical skincare product, supporting deep, restorative sleep directly benefits skin appearance. Many users notice improved complexion within weeks.

Q: Can men use Beauty Sleep Pill?

A: Absolutely! While marketed with "beauty" branding, the formula provides excellent sleep support for anyone. Quality sleep benefits everyone's appearance and health.

Q: What if I wake up during the night?

A: This formula is designed to support sleep maintenance, but if you wake, avoid checking your phone or clock. Practice relaxation breathing and allow sleep to return naturally. Don't take additional doses.

Q: How is this different from regular melatonin?

A: Beauty Sleep combines melatonin with multiple sleep-supporting ingredients plus beauty-enhancing nutrients. It's more comprehensive than melatonin alone, addressing multiple pathways to better sleep and overnight renewal.

Q: Can I use this for jet lag?

A: Yes, melatonin is well-established for jet lag. Take at target bedtime in your new time zone to help reset your circadian rhythm.

Q: Will I have vivid dreams?

A: Some people experience more vivid or memorable dreams, particularly from the B6 and 5-HTP. This is generally not disturbing and may actually be enjoyable. If dreams are bothersome, reduce dose slightly.
