What Causes Eye Dullness and Redness?

The whites of our eyes (sclera) can become discolored or red due to a range of factors:

  • Prolonged Screen Time: Blue light exposure from phones, computers, and TVs leads to eye strain and increased redness.
  • Environmental Irritants: Pollution, allergens, dust, and smoke can cause inflammation and dryness.
  • Lack of Sleep & Stress: Inadequate rest and stress response trigger blood vessel dilation, causing redness.
  • Underlying Health Conditions: Conditions like conjunctivitis, dry eye syndrome, or vitamin deficiencies impact sclera health.

Understanding and addressing these causes is critical to maintaining bright, healthy eyes.
