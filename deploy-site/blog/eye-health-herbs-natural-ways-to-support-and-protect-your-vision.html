In an age dominated by screens and digital devices, protecting your eyes is more important than ever. Fortunately, nature provides several powerful herbs known for their benefits to eye health. Incorporating these herbs into your daily routine can help reduce eye strain, support vision, and promote overall ocular wellness.

Bilberry

Bilberry is rich in antioxidants called anthocyanins, which improve blood circulation in the eyes and strengthen capillaries. It has been shown to enhance night vision and reduce symptoms of eye fatigue, making it an ideal choice for those frequently exposed to harsh lighting or prolonged screen time.

Eyebright

Traditionally used for eye complaints, eyebright contains anti-inflammatory properties that soothe irritated and tired eyes. Drinking eyebright tea or using it as a gentle compress can help reduce redness and inflammation, providing relief for dry, itchy eyes.

Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo biloba is known to improve blood flow, including to the delicate tissues of the eyes. This herb may help protect against age-related vision issues like glaucoma or macular degeneration by combating oxidative stress and promoting healthy circulation.
