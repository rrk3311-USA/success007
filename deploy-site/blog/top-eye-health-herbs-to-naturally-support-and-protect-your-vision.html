Incorporating these herbs into your daily routine can help protect your eyes and reduce discomfort caused by prolonged screen time and environmental factors.

Bilberry

Bilberry is celebrated for its antioxidant-rich profile, particularly anthocyanins, which help strengthen blood vessels in the eyes and improve retinal function. Studies suggest bilberry may enhance night vision and reduce eye fatigue, making it an excellent natural supplement for digital device users.

Eyebright

Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, eyebright helps soothe irritated and tired eyes. Whether used as an herbal tea or a gentle compress, eyebright can relieve redness and reduce inflammation, promoting overall eye comfort.

Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo biloba improves blood circulation, including to the eyes, which nourishes ocular tissues and helps protect against age-related issues like macular degeneration and glaucoma. Its antioxidant effects also combat free radical damage in eye cells.

Turmeric

Turmeric contains curcumin, a powerful anti-inflammatory compound that protects eyes from oxidative stress. Regular consumption may reduce symptoms of dry eyes and support long-term eye health.

For optimal eye health, combine these herbs with a balanced diet rich in vitamins A, C, and E. Also, take breaks from screens regularly and protect your eyes from UV rays. By embracing these natural remedies, you can maintain healthy vision and reduce the risk of eye strain effectively.
