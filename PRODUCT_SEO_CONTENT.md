## SEO Best Practices Applied

### Title Tags (50-60 characters)
- Include primary keyword at the beginning
- Add brand name for recognition
- Use pipe separators for readability
- Stay under 60 characters for full display in search results

### Meta Descriptions (150-160 characters)
- Compelling call-to-action language
- Include primary and secondary keywords naturally
- Highlight unique selling points (USPs)
- Mention quantity/size for clarity
- Stay under 160 characters for full display

### Short Descriptions (150-200 words)
- Lead with benefits, not just features
- Include relevant keywords naturally
- Address customer pain points
- Highlight quality and certifications
- Use action-oriented language

### Image Alt Text
- Descriptive and keyword-rich
- Include brand name and product type
- Mention key features (quantity, size)
- Accessible for screen readers

## Implementation Notes

1. **Update Product Database:** Add these fields to your product records
2. **Shop Page Meta:** Use seo_title and meta_description in HTML head
3. **Product Cards:** Use short_description for product previews
4. **Images:** Apply image_alt to all product images
5. **Schema Markup:** Consider adding Product schema with this data
6. **URL Structure:** Ensure clean URLs with product names/SKUs
